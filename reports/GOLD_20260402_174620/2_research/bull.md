Here's what the bears are completely missing: **$1.70 billion in deferred revenue**. That's a **revenue pipeline that dwarfs the current market cap of $1.16 billion**. When that deferred revenue flows through the income statement — and it will — the earnings picture transforms dramatically. The current 85x PE looks expensive until you realize earnings are about to be supercharged by this backlog.

The macro report tells us clearly: **forced liquidations and liquidity pressures** across precious metals markets. Gold is on track for its worst monthly decline since 2008 — not because the underlying business broke down, but because investors needed cash and sold what they could. 

This is **textbook oversold capitulation**. RSI hit 24.6 — an extreme reading. Price tagged the Bollinger Lower Band. The MACD histogram has been improving for three consecutive weeks. The panic selling is **exhausting itself**.
